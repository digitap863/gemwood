Plywood -

Item Description

SUNPLY commercial plywood variants are manufactured using specially selected and vacuum pressure treated hardwood, Eucalyptus and Gurjan veneer. This plywood is moisture/water resistant and has anti warping qualities. Thickness dimensions and tolerance are according to IS: 12040: 1987 and confirms to IS: 303.
